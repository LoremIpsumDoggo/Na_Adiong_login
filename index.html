About this advocacy

The advocacy is named “National Association of Consumer Advocates” or “NACA”. They deal with the economic aspect of identity theft – like being able to open bank accounts with the victim’s information – by having attorneys stand for you.

More help is welcome because identity theft is a dangerous occurence, especially in this age of information.
